Ways to Partner

Industry Collaboration

For companies & startups

Partner with APC Research to co-develop nanotechnology solutions for your industry challenges.

  • Access to state-of-the-art characterization facilities
  • Customized R&D engagements with IP negotiation
  • Pilot testing in controlled lab environments
  • Dedicated researcher allocated to your project
  • Joint grant application support

Academic Partnership

For universities & research institutes

We actively seek co-authorship, student exchange, and joint research programs.

  • Bilateral student and researcher exchange programs
  • Co-authored publications in high-impact journals
  • Shared access to experimental infrastructure
  • Joint proposal development for international grants
  • Annual joint symposium and seminar series

International Research Network

For global institutions

APC Research is committed to positioning Nano Center Indonesia as a global hub for nanoscience.

  • MoU and consortium membership opportunities
  • Research visit programs for international scientists
  • Data-sharing and open-science collaboration
  • Co-hosting international conferences in Indonesia
  • Visibility on joint publication platforms

Contract Research Services

For organizations needing specific outputs

Need specific material characterization, testing, or applied research without a long-term commitment?

  • Material synthesis and characterization services
  • Environmental sample analysis and remediation testing
  • Technical feasibility studies and white papers
  • Confidential project execution with NDA
  • Rapid turnaround on standardized testing protocols
