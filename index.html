Key Members: Dr. Budi Santoso, Ratna Dewi

Nanofibers via Electrospinning

Synthesizing polymer nanofibers for biomedical and filtration applications.

Ongoing Projects:
  • Antibacterial Wound Dressings

Nano-Petro for Oil Extraction

Deploying nanofluids for enhanced oil recovery (EOR) and reservoir simulation.

Low-Dimensional Materials
